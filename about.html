Welcome to Our Story

Our Ethos

Our Goal, prayer and commitment

Our ethos is based on problem solving, decision making and self-help while promoting inter-personal communications. These are key elements which contribute to the child's development, self-esteem and confidence.

Our Commitment

At TPS we are committed to providing a fun-filled, rewarding learning experience that recognizes each child's individuality and balances their intellectual, social, physical, emotional, and creative needs

Our Prayer

Our Vision

To be the foundation school of choice for character development and academic excellence

Our Mission

To offer values-based education that develops in our learners' faith, character and critical thinking to meet the challenges of tomorrow, becoming global citizens capable of utilizing resources and technology responsibly for the benefit of the society.

Our Staff

Meet our dedicated team of educators

Our team consists of highly qualified and experienced educators who are passionate about nurturing young minds. With over 50 dedicated staff members, including teachers, teaching assistants, and support staff, we maintain an excellent student-to-teacher ratio that ensures personalized attention for every child.

  • Qualified Teachers: All our teachers are certified education professionals
  • Continuous Development: Regular training and professional development programs
  • Diverse Expertise: Specialists in early childhood education, arts, sports, and special needs
  • Passionate Team: Committed to creating a positive learning environment
